Knowledge Engineering and Data Science



The aim of the program is to offer a comprehensive training program combining modules in mathematics/statistics, computer science and artificial intelligence. It aims to provide students with the skills needed and demanded by the job market, to manage and analyze data, and to master concepts such as exploratory data analysis, statistical inference and modeling, machine learning and high-dimensional data analysis.



Admission requirements

Access to the first year of the Engineering Cycle is open to :
  • Candidates who have passed the National Common Entrance Examination (CNC) for Morocco’s engineering schools.
  • By examination for holders of a DEUG in Mathematical and Computer Sciences (SMI).
  • By examination for holders of the “Informatiste” diploma (Bac+4) or equivalent, with a scientific or technical baccalaureate.
