Engineering of Digital Information



The aim of the program is to provide technical and professional training in the digital information professions. It offers students wishing to deepen their knowledge and know-how in computer science the qualifications and skills needed to work in the new information-documentation professions, at the frontier between computer science and information science. The training content for this program focuses on the following new concepts and disciplines: Digital transformation, Data governance, Business intelligence, Artificial intelligence, Information management.



Admission requirements

Access to the first year of the Engineering Cycle is open to :
  • Candidates who have passed the National Common Entrance Examination (CNC) for Morocco’s engineering schools.
  • By examination for holders of a DEUG in Mathematical and Computer Sciences (SMI).
  • By examination for holders of the “Informatiste” diploma (Bac+4) or equivalent, with a scientific or technical baccalaureate.
