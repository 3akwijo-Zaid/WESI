Engineering of Information System and Digital Transition



The aim of the program is to provide Moroccan organizations and society with highly-qualified technological profiles capable of working in these fields, fulfilling the associated professions and functions, and adapting to the changes and mutations that are taking place. The content of the program takes account of the new changes that have given rise to new concepts, and even new fields and disciplines: Digital society , Information society , Business intelligence , Information Systems governance , Ubiquitous systems , Digital transformation , Knowledge economy. ,



Admission requirements

Access to the first year of the Engineering Cycle is open to :
  • Candidates who have passed the National Common Entrance Examination (CNC) for Morocco’s engineering schools.
  • By examination for holders of a DEUG in Mathematical and Computer Sciences (SMI).
  • By examination for holders of the “Informatiste” diploma (Bac+4) or equivalent, with a scientific or technical baccalaureate.
