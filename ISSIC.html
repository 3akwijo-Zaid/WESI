Engineering of Information System Security and Cybersecurity



This program is designed to meet the growing need to train cybersecurity engineers who will be able to respond effectively to the security issues faced by companies, thanks to a comprehensive training program combining the fundamentals of digital transformation and data science with the cutting-edge technical and managerial components of cybersecurity. The main technical skills to be acquired are: IS and network architecture , IS security architecture , IS security risk management , ISMS implementation and management , Security standards , Identity and access management , Cryptographic techniques , IS security governance and audit , Protection tools , Application security , IoT and Cloud security.



Admission requirements

Access to the first year of the Engineering Cycle is open to :
  • Candidates who have passed the National Common Entrance Examination (CNC) for Morocco’s engineering schools.
  • By examination for holders of a DEUG in Mathematical and Computer Sciences (SMI).
  • By examination for holders of the “Informatiste” diploma (Bac+4) or equivalent, with a scientific or technical baccalaureate.
